🏎️ Jallen Racing

Jallen Racing is competing in its first sim racing competition, the NLS24 — a challenging 24-hour endurance race at the legendary Nuremberg Ring. Our team of drivers includes Leni Hourahine, Xavier Wright, Gabe Wright, and Liam Chalmers, each bringing skill, strategy, and determination to the wheel. This race is in honour of our sunshine, beautiful boy John Allen, our god and protector, whose strength and spirit inspire every lap. His teachings of the Three Gates guide us through every challenge, his amazing Gold Swing motivates us to push further, and his well-known hatred for e-bikes keeps us laughing and focused. From preparation to pit stops, teamwork to speed, Jallen Racing embodies dedication, passion, and honor as we take on this incredible challenge together, celebrating his guidance, protection, and unstoppable courage at every turn.
